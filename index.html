Our Mission

Built by students,
for students.

LYCC is a student-run business case competition designed to give high schoolers a taste of real-world problem solving, strategic thinking, and professional presenting.

Real-World Problems

Case challenges are modelled on actual business dilemmas faced by companies today — from supply chain disruption to go-to-market strategy and sustainability decisions.

Day 1 · Learning & Case Release

Opening ceremony gathers everyone together. Teams receive the case, enjoy provided snacks, and begin brainstorming before continuing their work independently.

Day 2 · Presentations & Finals

Teams return at their scheduled time to present solutions to a panel of judges. Top teams advance to finals, where strategies are evaluated and winners are selected.

No Experience Needed

LYCC is designed to be accessible. Whether you're new to business concepts or already experienced, the competition provides a supportive environment to learn and compete.

How It Works

The Competition

One day. One case. Your best thinking. Here's exactly how the day unfolds.

Registration Opens — Summer 2026
Form Your Team
Assemble a team of 3 to 5 high school students. If you do not have a team, sign up individually and we will pair you with others. No prior business experience required — just curiosity and a willingness to think critically.
Saturday — Opening Ceremony
Receive the Case
Teams receive a real business scenario at the start of the day. So it's time to build your solution.
Sunday Afternoon — Case Day
Present to Judges
Each team delivers a 15-minute presentation followed by a Q&A session. Judges evaluate clarity, strategy, creativity, and delivery.
Sunday Evening — Case Day
Awards & Closing
Winners are announced and prizes are awarded. All participants receive feedback from judges and connect with mentors and sponsors.
01

Eligibility

Open to all high school students. Teams must have 3–5 members from the same or different schools.

02

The Case

Cases are realistic business problems. You might advise a startup on pricing, help a company enter a new market, or tackle a sustainability challenge.

03

Judging Criteria

Teams are scored on analysis depth, solution viability, presentation quality, and how well you handle judge questions.

04

Prizes

Details coming soon. Prizes will be awarded to top-placing teams, with recognition for best presentation, most creative solution, and more.
